Learn Japanese vocabulary through reading, not flashcards.

Tired of flashcards?
Staring at word lists,
repeating the same cards,
memorizing today and forgetting tomorrow?

Many Japanese learners face the same problems. Words are hard to remember. Studying feels boring and mechanical. Even if a word is memorized, it is difficult to recognize it in real sentences.

A different approach

Yomio takes a different approach.

Instead of memorizing words in isolation, Yomio helps you learn Japanese vocabulary by reading meaningful context. Words are learned naturally by encountering them again and again in real usage.

With Yomio, vocabulary is learned through simple tapping and reading.

How it works

Yomio keeps track of your learning status for each word. With just one tap, it selects words you are currently learning and generates a short paragraph at your level.

You can choose different styles such as short stories, diaries, or news. After reading, you simply report whether the words felt familiar.

Based on your feedback, Yomio automatically updates your learning progress and prepares the next reading for you. You do not need to manage anything yourself.

Learn naturally

By meeting words repeatedly in different contexts, you begin to recognize them faster and understand how they are actually used. Vocabulary gradually becomes more familiar without forcing yourself to memorize.

Yomio is designed to be simple and calm. There are no endless flashcards and no complicated settings. You just tap, read, and let Yomio handle the rest.

If you believe that language is learned through reading and real usage, not drills, Yomio is made for you.
